Brief Profile

Perfect Touch Salon and Spa a registered legal entity with Corporate Affairs Commission, (C.A.C), Abuja opened to the general public on March 20,2015 to provide Unisex salon service, health and wellness (Spa) service and training with an Admin. Manager, two Barbers, two Hair stylists and a Massage /Skin Care therapist opposite Osun Mall and beside Glo office, Olaiya area, Osogbo Osun State. Team Lead and Educators are Damilola Dosunmu, an Accountant and Make up Artist, Molade Kareem , a graduate of Osun Sate University and a therapist and Kafayat Muftau, a graduate of Mathematics from prestigious Obafemi Awolowo University and an Aesthetician . Today, the business, in addition to training of numerous youth , has successfully trained over 40 OYES cadets and 21 cadets are being trained in the art of beauty and well ness. Besides, it presently partner and provides direct livelihood to thirty two hitherto unemployed youth (ladies and gentle men) in three locations ( Osun Mall shop 1 & 6, Aenon Suites and Hotels , GRA Osogbo )and indirect means of livelihood to numerous others in the State of Osun.

Success Story

Perfect Touch Salon and Spa, since its establishment has successfully:

  • Obtained National Youth Service Corp-Skill Acquisition and Entrepreneurship Development( NYSC-SAED) accreditation as a Facilitator
  • Trained over 20 Corp members in various beauty and wellness skills
  • Trained 40 Osun Youth Empowerment Scheme (OYES) Cadets and Cadets are currently undergoing training in various beauty and wellness skills
  • Won an award as Best Stylist of the year in the State of Osun
